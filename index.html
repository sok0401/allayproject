ALLDAY PROJECT (ADP) is a 5-member group that debuted on June 23, 2025. Unlike typical project groups, ADP is a permanent powerhouse formed under THEBLACKLABEL.Bringing together industry veterans, dance prodigies, and fresh talents, they have redefined the genre with their hit debut single "FAMOUS" and the chart-topping "WICKED".
